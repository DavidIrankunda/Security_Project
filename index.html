FIA Awards Ceremony 2024

Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA)

The prestigious FIA Awards Ceremony, hosted for the first time on African soil, celebrated the world’s motorsport champions in grand style. Held at Kigali’s BK Arena, this landmark event welcomed VIPs and global leaders, marking the FIA’s 120th anniversary. Crowd Minders played a pivotal role in securing the night’s success with unmatched professionalism and expertise.

  • Client Name: Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA)

  • Date:December 13, 2024

  • Location: BK Arena, Kigali, Rwanda

Event Highlights
Event Type: Global Motorsport Awards Ceremony

Key Details: For the first time in history, the FIA Awards Ceremony was held on African soil. Kigali’s BK Arena hosted this monumental celebration of motorsport excellence, with high-profile attendees including global leaders, VIPs, and the sport’s top talents. The event marked the FIA’s 120th anniversary and honored champions from various motorsport disciplines.

Challenges Addressed: Coordinating security for international dignitaries, managing tight schedules, and facilitating a world-class experience for attendees.
Global Motorsport Leaders: The event brought together key figures, including FIA President Mohammed Ben Sulayem and motorsport legends.

Historic Celebrations: Highlights included honoring Formula 1 Champion Max Verstappen, who secured his fourth consecutive title in 2024.

Regional Impact: The ceremony underscored Rwanda’s growing status as a hub for global events, with discussions of hosting a future Formula One Grand Prix.

Our Contributions
Event Security Services:Provided top-notch security measures to protect guests and ensure smooth operations in every part of the event.
Professional Event Stewards: Strategically placed to control access points and ensure that all areas were secure and well-managed.
Ushering & Liaison Services: Assisted attendees, including media representatives and VIP guests, in navigating the venue effortlessly.
Protocol & Guide Services: Managed VIP and dignitary movements with care, ensuring an exceptional experience for all attendees.
Emergency Support: Maintained a rapid-response team on-site, ensuring all risks were mitigated effectively.
