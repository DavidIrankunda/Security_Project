About Us

Crowd Minders is Rwanda’s leading security company, trusted by businesses, organizations, and event organizers since 2015. Founded on the principles of discipline, professionalism, and innovation, we specialize in providing reliable security solutions for events, venues, and private gatherings.

From managing massive crowds at Kigali Arena to protecting intimate corporate events, our expertise has made us a cornerstone of safety in Rwanda. Our team is composed of highly trained professionals with backgrounds in law enforcement, military, and advanced security practices.

Mission

To deliver exceptional security solutions that ensure safety, foster confidence, and contribute to the success of every event or venue we serve. At Crowd Minders, we don’t just protect people—we enable moments of connection, celebration, and growth by creating secure environments.
